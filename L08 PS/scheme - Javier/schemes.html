Health Schemes and Subsidies

Schemes

Scheme 1: MediSave

Description: MediSave is a national medical savings scheme that helps individuals set aside part of their income to pay for their personal or approved dependents’ hospitalisation, day surgery, and certain outpatient expenses, as well as their healthcare needs in old age.

Eligibility: Any CPF member who contributes to Medisave and has a positive Medisave balance.

Benefits: Can be used to pay for personal and family's hospitalisation.

Scheme 2: MediShield Life

Description: MediShield Life is a basic health insurance plan that helps to pay for large hospital bills and selected costly outpatient treatments, such as dialysis and chemotherapy for cancer.

Eligibility: All Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents are automatically covered by MediShield Life.

Benefits: Provides lifetime coverage, ensuring that you and your loved ones can always afford to pay for basic healthcare needs.

Subsidies

Subsidy

Description: Subsidies for Specialist Outpatient Care at Public Healthcare Institutions.

Eligibility: Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents receiving subsidised Specialist Outpatient Clinic care at Public Healthcare Institutions.

Benefits: Up to 70% subsidy for Singapore Citizens, 25% for Permanent Residents.

Consultation

A consultation with a doctor or other expert is a meeting with them to discuss a particular problem and get their advice. Consultation is the process of getting advice from a doctor or other expert. A personal diet plan is devised after a consultation with a nutritionist.

Short term care ward

A short-stay ward is a ward in an accident and emergency department of a restructured hospital for patients who need a short period of inpatient monitoring and treatment.

Long term care ward

LTC wards provide long-term care for older adults with severe physical and cognitive problems under the national healthcare insurance system. Patients in LTC wards are generally admitted from acute/subacute hospital wards after acute treatments, or from home due to exacerbation of their disease conditions.
